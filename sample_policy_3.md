# Increment Policy

## Overview
The Increment Policy outlines how salary increases are determined based on performance, inflation, and market adjustments.

## Eligibility
All permanent employees are eligible, subject to performance reviews and company guidelines.

## Frequency
Increments are typically annual, aligned with the performance appraisal cycle.

## Factors
Factors include individual performance, cost of living adjustments, and market competitiveness.

## Minimum Increment
A minimum increment is set to account for inflation and basic adjustments.

## Above Average Increments
High performers may receive above-average increments based on exceptional contributions.

## Below Expectations
Increments may be withheld or reduced for employees not meeting standards.

## Communication
Through performance review meetings and official salary revision letters.

## Impact on Benefits
Increments impact benefits like provident fund contributions and gratuity calculations.

## Appeals
Employees can discuss concerns with HR or managers; appeals are reviewed case-by-case.
